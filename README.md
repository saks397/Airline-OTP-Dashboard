# Airline On-Time Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview
A 3-page Power BI dashboard analyzing on-time performance 
across 121 million US flights from 2003 to 2022.

## Problem Statement
Airlines lose millions due to delays but lack a clear view 
of where and why delays happen.

## Key Insights
- Overall OTP: 80.92%
- 68% of delays are controllable internal issues
- Late aircraft cause 38% of all delays
- Weather causes only 5.2% of delays

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX
- Power Query
- US DOT Airline Dataset (Kaggle)

## Dashboard Pages
1. Executive Summary - KPI cards and OTP trend
2. Airport Analysis - Worst performing airports
3. Delay Causes - Breakdown of delay reasons

## Dataset
Source: US Department of Transportation
Records: 121 million flights
Period: 2003 - 2022
